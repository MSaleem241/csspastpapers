Federal Public Service Commission

Islamic Studies / Comparative Religions

Competitive Examination-2020

The 2020 Islamiat paper strongly emphasizes the practical ethical teachings of Islam through the life of the Holy Prophet (PBUH) and the early Islamic period. Topics such as tolerance, human rights, Jihad, the Madina Charter, and Islamic economic principles highlight Islam’s guidance for social justice, governance, and inter-human relations in contemporary society.
