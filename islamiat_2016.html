Federal Public Service Commission

Islamic Studies / Comparative Religions

Competitive Examination-2016

The 2016 Islamiat paper focuses strongly on foundational Islamic concepts and their practical application in governance and society. Questions cover Islamic culture, the concept of Deen, prayer, and the political and administrative system in Islam, particularly during the Khilafat-e-Rashida. The paper also links Islamic teachings with contemporary challenges facing the Muslim world.
