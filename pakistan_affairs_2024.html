Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-III (Pakistan Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2024

The 2024 Pakistan Affairs paper highlights governance, constitutional development, and internal stability as central themes. Questions emphasize political instability, civil–military relations, national integration, and equitable resource distribution. It also incorporates ideological history through Sheikh Ahmad Sirhindi and contemporary global concerns like climate change. Overall, the paper reflects a strong focus on structural reforms, democratic consolidation, and sustainable national development.

PART-II (Subjective)

Question No. 2

Political stability remains an elusive concept in Pakistan. Explore the factors contributing to the fractured nature of Pakistan's polity and discuss potential reforms to the constitutional and political structure that could foster stability.

Question No. 3

Keeping in view the socio-politico circumstances of sub-continent, discuss the role of Sheikh Ahmed Sarhindi (Mujadid Alf Thani) who revived Islamic Ideology and established Muslim identity in sub-continent.

Question No. 4

The Democratization process in Pakistan is still weak and ambiguous. Do you consider dynastic politics and feudalism are the major hindrances on the way of establishing true democratic system in Pakistan? Explain.

Question No. 5

Global warming presents an existential threat to states like Pakistan. Examine the far-reaching impacts of global warming on Pakistan and propose a model of economic development that aligns with environmental protection.

Question No. 6

How should Pakistan manage the equitable distribution of resources among different ethnic regions to address historical grievances and promote developments across the country?

Question No. 7

In Modern day democracy there must be a Pragmatic Civil-Military relationship to deal all the internal and external security threats. Analyze with reference to the role of Pakistan’s security and stability in South-Asia.

Question No. 8

Since Independence, the most critical concern for Internal security is National Integration. Discuss the role of constitutional provisions enabling provincial autonomy and devolution of Power to strengthen National integration in Pakistan?
