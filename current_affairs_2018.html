Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-II (Current Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2018

The CSS 2018 Current Affairs paper appears to focus on Pakistan’s strategic and economic positioning in a rapidly changing global environment, while also testing knowledge of international diplomacy, security issues, and regional conflicts. The MCQs touch on topics like nuclear deals, international organizations, CPEC, and historical milestones, suggesting that candidates needed to be aware of both global and regional developments. Subjective questions emphasize economic corridors, energy projects, military alliances, and US-China rivalry, alongside regional crises such as the Qatar diplomatic standoff. Overall, the paper seems aimed at evaluating both factual awareness and analytical thinking about Pakistan’s role in regional and global affairs.
