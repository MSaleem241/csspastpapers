Political Science I-II

Competitive Examination-2016

The 2016 Political Science paper reflects a strong blend of classical political thought and modern political systems. It covers key thinkers like Aristotle, Niccolò Machiavelli, and Al-Mawardi, alongside contemporary concepts such as democracy, federation, and civil society. The second paper emphasizes comparative politics, including systems of the United States, United Kingdom, France, and Pakistan. Overall, it highlights a balanced approach between theory and practical governance in global and regional contexts.
