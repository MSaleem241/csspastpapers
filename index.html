About CSS & FPSC

The Central Superior Services (CSS) is the elite permanent bureaucracy of Pakistan, managed by the Federal Public Service Commission (FPSC). It is the most competitive examination in the country, selecting officers for the top 12 administrative groups.

At CSSPASTPAPERS.COM, we understand that practicing past papers is the most critical part of preparation. We have curated an organized database of Past Papers from 2016 to 2025 and Repeating Questions, allowing you to track exam trends and master the syllabus with ease.
