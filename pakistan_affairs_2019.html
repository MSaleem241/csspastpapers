Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-III (Pakistan Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2019

The 2019 Pakistan Affairs paper strongly emphasizes contemporary socio-economic challenges. While one question revisits pre-partition politics, most focus on governance issues such as economic instability, population growth, energy crisis, water management, and resource utilization. This indicates a growing trend in CSS to test candidates’ understanding of Pakistan’s developmental problems and their ability to propose practical policy solutions.

A. Rs 5.55 tn | B. Rs 4.75 tn | C. Rs 4.90 tn | D. Rs 6.55 tn
