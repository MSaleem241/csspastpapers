PESTEL Formula (APPLIED)

PESTEL Applied to a CSS Essay Topic

Topic:
“Cyber Security as the New National Security Frontier”

Political Dimension

• Cyber warfare between states (e.g., espionage, hacking)
• Threat to national sovereignty through digital attacks
• Need for national cyber policies and strategies
• Role of intelligence agencies in cyber defense
Argument:
Cyber security has become a political necessity as states face invisible digital threats that
challenge sovereignty.

Economic Dimension

• Financial losses due to cybercrime
• Threats to banking systems and digital economy
• Impact on investor confidence
• Cost of cyber defense infrastructure
Argument:
The rise of cyber threats poses serious economic risks by destabilizing financial systems
and discouraging investment.

Social Dimension

• Data privacy concerns for citizens
• Spread of misinformation and propaganda
• Psychological impact of digital manipulation
• Public trust erosion
Argument:
Cyber insecurity undermines social stability by enabling misinformation and compromising
personal data.

Technological Dimension

• Dependence on digital systems
• Growth of AI-driven cyber attacks
• Weak cyber infrastructure in developing states
• Need for innovation in cyber defense
Argument:
Rapid technological advancement has expanded the cyber threat landscape, making
digital defense a core security priority.

Environmental Dimension (Creative Angle )

• Cyber attacks on energy grids
• Disruption of water and environmental monitoring systems
• Threat to smart cities and infrastructure
Argument:
Cyber threats extend to environmental systems, where attacks on critical infrastructure
can disrupt essential services.

Legal Dimension

• Lack of international cyber laws
• Jurisdiction issues in cybercrime
• Weak enforcement mechanisms
• Need for global cyber governance
Argument:
The absence of strong legal frameworks limits effective control over cyber threats at both
national and international levels.
