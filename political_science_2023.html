Competitive Examination-2023

The 2023 Political Science paper reflects a blend of classical philosophy and contemporary governance challenges. It includes thinkers like Jeremy Bentham, Al-Ghazali, and Allama Muhammad Iqbal, alongside modern concepts such as bureaucracy, civil-military relations, and globalization. The paper also emphasizes regional cooperation through SAARC and highlights Pakistan’s political and economic challenges, showing a clear trend of integrating theory with current affairs.
