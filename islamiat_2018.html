Federal Public Service Commission

Islamic Studies / Comparative Religions

Competitive Examination-2018

The 2018 Islamiat paper highlights both classical Islamic concepts and their relevance to modern society. Topics such as Ijtihad, self-purification, Zakat, and Islamic civilization emphasize foundational teachings, while questions on terrorism and responsibilities of civil servants connect Islamic principles with contemporary social and governance issues. The trend shows a balanced focus on ideology, ethics, and practical application of Islam.
