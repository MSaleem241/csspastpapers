Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-III (Pakistan Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2021

The 2021 Pakistan Affairs paper strongly emphasizes contemporary geopolitical and security issues. Questions focus on Kashmir after August 2019, the Afghanistan peace process, CPEC Phase II, renewable energy, and fifth-generation warfare. Alongside these modern themes, the paper retains ideological grounding through Muslim separate identity. Overall, it reflects a trend toward linking historical foundations with current regional security and economic challenges.
