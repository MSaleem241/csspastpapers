CSS General Science & Ability | Repeating Questions

Competitive Examination-2016-2025

A review of the past decade’s CSS General Science & Ability papers shows a clear pattern of thematic consistency and repetition. Environmental issues such as climate change, pollution, and global warming appear almost every year. Human biology topics like vaccines, hepatitis, and nutrition are frequently tested, while technology-related subjects including artificial intelligence, GPS, and computer fundamentals have become regular features. Physics and space science also recur with minor variations. The analytical mathematics section follows a stable structure annually. Overall, the trend suggests that success lies in mastering core concepts rather than expecting entirely new or unpredictable topics.
