Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-III (Pakistan Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2020

The 2020 Pakistan Affairs paper presents a balanced mix of historical, political, and contemporary security issues. It begins with Muslim reform movements and gradually shifts toward foreign policy, regional diplomacy, and internal security. Topics like SCO, the Simla Agreement, proxy wars, and Pak-US relations highlight geopolitical concerns, while political culture and the energy crisis reflect ongoing governance and policy challenges.
