Federal Public Service Commission

Islamic Studies / Comparative Religions

Competitive Examination-2021

The 2021 Islamiat paper emphasizes Islamic beliefs, social harmony, and governance principles. Questions focus on the Day of Judgment, gender rights, accountability of rulers, and peaceful coexistence in multi-faith societies. It also addresses contemporary issues like Islamophobia and highlights the role of Sufism in spreading Islam, reflecting a strong link between Islamic teachings and modern global challenges.
