Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-II (Current Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2019

The CSS 2019 Current Affairs paper seems to revolve around a combination of Pakistan-focused governance, security, and foreign policy issues, along with major international developments and environmental concerns. From the factual MCQs about historical events, global organizations, and infrastructure projects, to subjective questions on the Belt and Road Initiative, energy policy, hybrid warfare, and climate agreements, it appears the paper emphasizes Pakistan’s strategic positioning amid global challenges. There is a noticeable focus on security, regional conflicts, and resource management, as well as governance and anti-corruption measures. Overall, it seems designed to test both factual awareness and analytical thinking about Pakistan’s domestic and international affairs.
