Federal Public Service Commission

Islamic Studies / Comparative Religions

Competitive Examination-2019

The 2019 Islamiat paper emphasizes core Islamic beliefs and their relevance to contemporary social and political issues. Questions focus on Tauheed, moral values, Hajj, and the character of the Prophet (PBUH), while also addressing modern debates such as women’s rights, extremism, and political reform in light of Islamic principles. The trend reflects linking classical teachings with modern societal challenges.
