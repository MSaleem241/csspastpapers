General Knowledge-II (Current Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2024

The CSS 2024 Current Affairs paper seems to be largely centered around themes of international relations, regional geopolitics, and Pakistan’s foreign policy orientation, though it also touches upon domestic governance issues. It appears to emphasize understanding of key global events—such as the Palestine conflict, Ukraine war, and great power dynamics—while linking them to Pakistan’s strategic interests. At the same time, topics like provincial restructuring and regional cooperation suggest an overlap between internal and external policy dimensions. Overall, the paper seems to lean toward analytical and opinion-based responses, requiring candidates to interpret global developments and evaluate their implications rather than relying solely on factual recall.
