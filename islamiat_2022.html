Islamic Studies / Comparative Religions

Competitive Examination-2022

The 2022 Islamiat paper emphasizes foundational Islamic doctrines alongside their application to modern societal issues. Questions focus on human rights, the status of women, Islamic economics, Ijtihad, and the concept of Prophethood. It also highlights Islam’s role in promoting peace and guiding social reconstruction, reflecting a trend of linking core beliefs with contemporary global and societal challenges.
