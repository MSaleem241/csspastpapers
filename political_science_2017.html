Political Science I-II

Competitive Examination-2017

The 2017 Political Science paper reflects a strong emphasis on classical political theory and its relevance to modern governance. It highlights thinkers like Thomas Hobbes, John Locke, Karl Marx, and Ibn Khaldun, linking their ideas to contemporary political systems. The paper also focuses on comparative politics, including the United States and United Kingdom, alongside regional and national issues in Pakistan. Overall, it demonstrates a balanced integration of political thought, institutional analysis, and practical challenges in governance.
