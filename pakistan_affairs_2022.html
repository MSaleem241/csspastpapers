Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-III (Pakistan Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2022

The 2022 Pakistan Affairs paper focuses largely on political development, constitutional structure, and foreign policy. Questions highlight regional political parties, the 18th Amendment, early factional politics, and Pakistan’s foreign policy choices. It also examines economic dependence on foreign aid and evolving Pakistan–US relations after the Afghanistan withdrawal, reflecting a strong emphasis on governance, political history, and strategic diplomacy.

PART-II (Subjective)

Question No. 2

Discuss the role of regional and nationalist political parties in Pakistani politics. How far these parties are necessary for the political system?

Question No. 3

Discuss the Federal Structure of 1973 Constitution of Pakistan after 18th Amendment. Why criticism on 18th Amendment started recently?

Question No. 4

Evaluate the factional politics of early years(1947-58) and its impact on the democratic process of Pakistan.

Question No. 5

Every state designs its foreign policy on its National interests rejecting feelings and emotions. Why did Pakistan prefer emotions, feelings and Ideology in its foreign policy? Also analyze its impact.

Question No. 6

What is the volume of grants, aid and loans in Pakistan’s economy in the last ten years to stimulate the growth? Discuss.

Question No. 7

Why did Pakistan join Western Defense Pacts? What cost it had to pay for that? Explain.

Question No. 8

Discuss the issues and mistrust in US-Pakistan relations after the withdrawal of the US troops from Afghanistan.
