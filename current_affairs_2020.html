General Knowledge-II (Current Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2020

The CSS 2020 Current Affairs paper seems to be focused on the intersection of global geopolitics, regional security, and Pakistan’s strategic and economic challenges, though it also includes environmental and institutional issues. It appears to emphasize major international developments, including climate change, Indo-Pacific power dynamics, FATF obligations, Kashmir, and strategic alliances like NATO and OIC, suggesting a concern with Pakistan’s position in a shifting global order. At the same time, questions on water resources, inter-provincial harmony, and economic measures indicate attention to domestic implications of global trends. Overall, the paper seems to combine factual knowledge with analytical reasoning, encouraging candidates to link global events with national policy considerations.

PART-II (Subjective)

Question No. 2

What impact global climate change will have on the water resources of Pakistan? How will it affect inter-provincial harmony?

Question No. 3

Why was Pakistan placed on the ‘Grey List’ of Financial Action Task Force (FATF)? What are the implications and what measures should Pakistan take to move out of this list?

Question No. 4

Given the volatile lockdown in Indian Occupied Kashmir, what are the viable policy choices available to Pakistan? What can Pakistan realistically do to ease the sufferings of Kashmiri people?

Question No. 5

Discuss the strategic contours of Indo-US evolving partnership and how will it impact Pakistan-US and Pakistan-China relations?.

Question No. 6

Strategic competition in the Indo-Pacific region is increasingly becoming intense. Define the interests and role of US, China, India, Japan and Australia in the geopolitics of Indo-Pacific region.

Question No. 7

Discuss in detail the role of OIC, Arab league and GCC in the Middle East crises and conflicts.

Question No. 8

In view of the evolving global alignments and changing nature of major-power relationships, what is the future of globalism and multilateralism? Discuss with examples.
