The CSS 2025 Current Affairs paper seems to revolve largely around themes of global politics, strategic affairs, and Pakistan’s policy challenges, though it is not limited to a single domain. It appears to place significant emphasis on linking international developments—such as global alliances, security frameworks, and economic shifts—with Pakistan’s internal realities, including economic instability, social cohesion, and environmental concerns. Rather than focusing purely on factual knowledge, the paper seems to lean toward testing analytical thinking and the ability to form connections between global and domestic issues, suggesting a broader, more interpretative approach to current affairs.
