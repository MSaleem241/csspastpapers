Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-III (Pakistan Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2025

The 2025 Pakistan Affairs paper presents a balanced combination of historical events, economic policies, and contemporary national issues. It revisits key historical topics like the Indus Waters Treaty and the 1971 separation, while also emphasizing economic development through CPEC and Bhutto’s nationalization. Additionally, themes of national integration, Kashmir policy, and media influence highlight growing attention to governance and modern socio-political dynamics.

PART-II (Subjective)
