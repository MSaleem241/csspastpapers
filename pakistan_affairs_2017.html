Federal Public Service Commission

General Knowledge-III (Pakistan Affairs)

Competitive Examination-2017

The 2017 Pakistan Affairs paper shows a clear shift toward governance, institutional development, and emerging regional connectivity. Unlike purely historical focus, it emphasizes judiciary’s constitutional role, democratic consolidation, higher education reforms, and energy security. Simultaneously, CPEC and SCO highlight Pakistan’s evolving geo-economic orientation. Overall, the trend reflects a transition from ideological debates to policy performance, development challenges, and regional integration.
